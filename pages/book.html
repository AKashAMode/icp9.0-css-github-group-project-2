Lions are large felines found in Africa and India. Known as the "king of the jungle," they are social animals that live in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their impressive trunks, large ears, and tusks, and are found in Africa and Asia.

Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds that live in the Southern Hemisphere, primarily in Antarctica. They are excellent swimmers and have adapted to life in the water.

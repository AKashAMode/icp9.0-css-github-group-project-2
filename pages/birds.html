Mallard Ducks

                                                                        
Mallard Duck

Common Name: Mallard Duck

Scientific Name: Anas platyrhynchos

Location: Northern Hemisphere.

Life Span: 5 to 10 years

Fact: Mallards swim with their tail held above the water.
